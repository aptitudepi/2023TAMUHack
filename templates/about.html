About Us

STEAM Life is a student-led project dedicated to promoting the Allen ISD STEAM Center. Established in 2020, the project started under the STEAM Ambassadors but has since expanded into Computer Science and Graphic Design. Here you can find up to date information about the STEAM Center gathered by students, for students.

Mission Statement

We started STEAM Life because we realized how difficult it could be to find STEAM-specific information in our community, especially for current and prospective students. That's why our project began with the goal to streamline information and updates about the STEAM Center. Since then, we have been Allen ISD approved, and we currently are working on an app version of STEAM Life with more personalized features. Going forward we will keep promoting the STEAM Center and dedicate ourselves to serving the community. Feel free to reach out to us if you have any suggestions for improvement.
